Greek Mythology

Origins

Greek mythology has complicated origins. For the most part, Greek mythology started from oral traditions during the bronze age. These traditions eventually evolved into written text throughout the archaic and classical periods.

Legacy

The legacy of the Greek mythos is most celebrated with Homer's works - the Illiad and the Odyssey. These tales of the Trojan Wars cemented Greek culture into the minds of modern people as an illustrious society filled with intense and impossible feats of success.
